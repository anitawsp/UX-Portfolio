Healthy Lemon

A recipe application for healthy foods!

People always struggle between eating healthy and tasting good. Do you know that a healthy diet can also be tasty? What is the way that you get your healthy food? Are you preparing them by yourself?...During the pandemic, we spend a lot of time staying at home and want to find ways out of getting bored. If you want to prepare healthy food for yourself or your family, try “Healthy Lemon” — A mobile recipe application showing that our diet can not only be healthy but also tasty!

Black Dog and Co.

Unlimited love to our fluffy friends!

Black Dog And Company is a course project. I designed the logo for this animal rescue organization, meaning “paw inside a warm house”. I also designed their real time marketing social media campaigns for Instagram platform. On the landing page, the header is the campaign image, with a “call-to-action” button, the website is fun and engaging to visitors. Overall web color palette is warm, trotical, and happy, showing our love and passion to the animals!
